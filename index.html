The Array Game

Score: 0

How to Play

  • This game is about mental speed and pattern recognition.
  • An array of numbers will appear on the board.
  • You and the AI take turns mentally removing the highest number and all numbers to its right, until the array is empty.
  • If you empty the array on your turn, the array is considered "winning".
  • Your goal is to predict whether the array is "winning" or "losing".
  • For example, in the array 5, 4, 10, 9, 8, 12, 6: You remove 12 and 6. The AI removes 10, 9, and 8. You then remove 5 and 4, winning the game.
  • In another array 8, 6, 9, 1: You remove 9 and 1. The AI removes 8 and 6, so the AI finishes the array, meaning the array is "losing".
  • Place your bet before time runs out!
  • Choose your difficulty (Easy, Medium, Hard, Hero) for more excitement and bigger rewards.
Good Luck!
